Welcome to SafeHearts

Heart disease is the leading cause of death for men, women, and people of most racial and ethnic groups in the USA and the second in Canada. Every hour 12 Canadian adults age 20+ with diagnosed heart disease die. The early detection can help reducing the risk of heart disease. SafeHearts applies machine learning techniques to detect hidden patterns and to predict the presence of heart disease.
